Whenever I go for a drive in the countryside there are always a few things I do before setting off. First of all, I sit in the driver's seat of my car and put my hands on the
steering wheel
to make sure it is secure. Next, I put the keys in the ignition and switch on the
headlights
. Sometimes it is dark in the countryside, so it is important that the lights work. After this, I make sure that the
windscreen wipers
are working. It rains often where I live and without these I would not be able to see out of the
windscreen
. The next thing I do is to open the
bonnet
and check the oil and water levels. Once I am satisfied that my car is functioning correctly, I pick up my lunch and store it in the
boot
. Finally, I sit back in the driver's seat, fasten my
seatbelt
,put my foot on the
accelerator
and head off down the road.
